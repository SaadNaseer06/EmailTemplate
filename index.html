2024 Q2 INVESTMENT REVIEW

Five years ago, J-F Démoré and Cliff Richardson joined forces to launch Northern Ontario’s first pooled fund at Innova. Fueled by a mix of greed and fear, they aimed to offer more than what was available for their own portfolios while providing solutions to mitigate sequence of return and emotional risks for clients. Their objective was clear: earn more than the 4% return required by a prudent retirement plan with minimal volatility. As of June 30th, 2024, their 5-year annualized rate of return, calculated by a third-party, stood at 6.26%.

The Innova Tactical Asset Allocation Private Pool (ITAAP) was designed to control portfolio volatility by diversifying beyond traditional stocks and bonds. Over the past five years, the Tactical Pool has reduced drawdowns during downturns and slightly outperformed peers. This success has led to the launch of the Enhanced Growth fund and Dedicated Defensive strategy.

Innova remains dedicated to transparency and delivering value through consistent returns and managed volatility. The team expresses deep appreciation for the trust clients have placed in them and looks forward to continuing to exceed investment objectives together. Thank you for your partnership.
